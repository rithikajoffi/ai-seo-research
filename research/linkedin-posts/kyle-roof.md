## Post 1 — February 2026

Not all SEO content is the same - and neither is the way we deliver it.

Inside POP White Glove, every client gets a content silo made of:

- Top Level Content → The "parent" page for your main keyword (service, location, product).
- Supporting Content → Articles that strengthen the Top Level page's authority and rankings.

Then, you choose how you want that content delivered:

1. **Content Brief / Outline** — We create a marked-up page outline, schema, and instructions for your writers to execute.
2. **Existing Page Update Brief** — For published pages, we give you a detailed brief to re-optimize - includes schema & instructions.
3. **AI-Generated Draft** — We deliver a full draft fast, using AI + POP optimization. Your team handles the final proofing.
4. **Human-Edited Content** — We write and fact-check the page, optimizing it for structure, accuracy, and SEO.

The goal is to give you exactly the right content format for your workflow - whether you have an in-house team or want us to handle everything.
