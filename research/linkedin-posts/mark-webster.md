## Post 2 — 2025

"Hi, I hope this message finds you in great spirits..." — DELETE.

If your link building outreach starts like this in 2024, you are burning money.

What these senders did wrong:
- Mass-blasted the same template to multiple team members
- Zero personalization or research
- Generic value proposition
- Starting with "hope this finds you well"
- Focused purely on what they want
- No understanding of the recipient, website or its audience

We've built 328 outreach backlinks (average DR 57) in the last 24 months. It still works — but the process has changed significantly.

What it looks like in practice:

1. **Research Their Website (5 minutes minimum)** — study the content, understand their audience, identify gaps.
2. **Find the Right Person (5 minutes)** — don't blast generic addresses. Research team members on LinkedIn.
3. **Learn About Your Prospect (5-10 minutes)** — read their recent posts, look for personal interests.
4. **Hyper-Personalized Outreach (10 minutes)** — reference specific content they've published. Make it about them, not you.

The secret isn't a magical template. It's putting in the work that 90% of link builders won't do.
