## Summary

Aleyda analyzed the biggest domain-level visibility shifts in Sistrix for the US and UK during the May 2026 Core Update (window: May 26 to June 2, 2026).

**5 key patterns from the update:**

1. **Source type fit mattered more than authority alone** — sites with the right content type for the query won, regardless of domain authority
2. **Forum and Q&A visibility contracted**, while social and video platforms were mixed to positive
3. **UK ecommerce visibility shifted toward local market entities** — international brands lost ground to local ones
4. **Jobs and travel marketplaces show "aggregators lost" is too simple** — some aggregators won, others lost, depending on intent match
5. **Health was sorted by confidence and result fit** — authoritative, specific health sources gained; generic health content lost

**Bottom line:** The May 2026 update reinforced a clear signal — Google is matching source type to search intent more precisely than ever. Having the right content format for the right query now matters as much as the content itself.
