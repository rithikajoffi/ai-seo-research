# Cyrus Shepard — 17 Content Types to Survive Google's Zero-Click Future

## Summary

Cyrus Shepard analyzed 400+ websites to identify what separates sites gaining organic traffic from those losing it. The study used Sistrix visibility data, Ahrefs API for 12-month traffic changes, and Spearman rank correlations.

**Core finding:** Sites with zero winning features had a 13.5% win rate. Sites with all five data-backed features reached a 69.7% win rate. The effects were additive.

**The 5 data-backed features of winning sites:**
1. Strong brand (measured by share of branded navigational searches in top keywords)
2. Task completion (the site helps users *do* something, not just read)
3. Proprietary assets (data, tools, or content AI cannot replicate)
4. Specific business model fit (transaction pages, directories, aggregators)
5. Creator/authority profile that signals real expertise

**Content tiers by resilience:**

- **Very strong:** Owned audience (email lists, communities) and transaction pages — these don't depend on Google at all
- **Strong:** Original research, first-person experience content, niche communities, in-depth reviews
- **Weaker:** Generic informational content, listicles, content AI can instantly replicate

**Why this matters for AI SEO:** Nearly 60% of Google searches now end without a click to third-party websites. The sites winning are not the ones with the most aggressive SEO — they're the ones with the most differentiated, functionally useful products and data.
