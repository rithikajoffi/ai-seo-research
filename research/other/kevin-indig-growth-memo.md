## Growth Intelligence Brief #19

A weekly briefing tracking organic search and AI visibility across thousands of domains in 20+ verticals.

**Key findings from this issue:**

- **SaaS visibility dropped -7.8% in 30 days**, erasing the prior issue's rebound. AWS (-22.7%), Oracle (-17.0%), Stripe (-16.8%), Atlassian (-15.6%), and GitHub (-14.2%) all fell in lockstep while their AI mentions grew 25 to 90%.
- **Jobs vertical posted +21.2%**, with Glassdoor (+64.2%) and ZipRecruiter (+53.5%) doing nearly all the work — Google appears to be routing more "company reviews" and "role salary" queries back to aggregators.
- **Local Search rose +44.0%**, almost entirely on Yelp, which gained more raw visibility points than any other site in the Index this month.
- **Review aggregators keep absorbing traffic** that used to go to original sources — Trustpilot (+39.5%) and Glassdoor (+64.2%) extend this pattern from the prior issue.

**The big picture:** SaaS brands are losing SEO visibility while gaining AI mentions — the "divergence economy" where organic traffic and AI citations move in opposite directions has resumed at a wider gap than any prior reading.
